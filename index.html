INTRODUCTION
The Parameswara Seva Samiti or PSS in short, is the Social service wing of the Sadhguru Sri Chittibabaji Charitable Trust of Seethanagaram in the East Godavari District Andhra Pradesh. Formed in 1975 as envisaged by the vision of Sadhguru Sri Chittibabaji, the PSS is at present working in
Three specific fields :
Medical Emergency Services
Natural farming
Running an efficient Gosala.

KAVACHAM

Kavacham is an ambulance service run by the PSS within the Seethanagaram Mandal area with around 25 villages as part of the medical emergency services.

NATURAL FARMING

Started in 2010, PSS has been spearheading an agrarian programme that seeks to rejuvenate the natural balance of soil fertility by streamlining herbicide brands called Jeevam and Jeevamrutham.

PALAKADALI GOSALA

A full fledged Cow Farm or Gosala is being run by PSS at Pedakondepudi village in the Seethanagaram Mandal..
